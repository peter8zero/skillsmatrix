# Skills Matrix — Calc Services Team

## Original Brief

This service is a group of coders who work on C# building pension calculations for our in-house pensions admin platform. They build the calcs from specs, write reusable code, setup unit tests, test driven development, understand pension specs, understand worked calc examples in spreadsheets and manually written out, proformas, talk to the admin team to help them perform UAT, and deliver calcs as part of project work, and support ticket fixes for schemes that are live.

Requirements:
- A skills matrix covering all the above
- Levels for each skill to measure competency
- An expandable option for each skill explaining what it means and how to improve

---

## Skill Categories

Grouped into 4 areas rather than a flat list — makes the matrix more navigable and helps identify where someone's strengths cluster.

### 1. Technical (C# & Engineering)
- C# Language & .NET Framework
- Reusable Code & Design Patterns
- Unit Testing & TDD
- SQL & Data Access
- Source Control & CI/CD
- Debugging & Troubleshooting

### 2. Pension Domain
- Pension Scheme Rules & Legislation
- Calculation Specifications (reading/interpreting)
- Worked Examples & Spreadsheet Calcs
- Proformas & Output Formats
- GMP / Equalisation / Revaluation (specialist topics)

### 3. Delivery & Process
- Project Delivery (estimates, planning, handover)
- Support Tickets & Live Issue Resolution
- UAT Support & Admin Team Collaboration
- Code Review (giving and receiving)
- Documentation & Knowledge Sharing

### 4. Professional
- Communication & Stakeholder Management
- Mentoring & Coaching
- Problem Solving & Initiative
- Time Management & Prioritisation

---

## Levels (1–4 Scale)

Four levels map cleanly to progression without agonising over middle scores:

| Level | Label | Meaning |
|-------|-------|---------|
| 1 | **Foundation** | Learning. Needs guidance and pairing. |
| 2 | **Practitioner** | Works independently on standard tasks. |
| 3 | **Advanced** | Handles complex/novel work. Coaches others. |
| 4 | **Expert** | Defines standards. Go-to person. Drives improvement. |

---

## Expandable Detail (Per Skill)

Each skill gets a panel showing:
- **What this skill means** — plain English description
- **What each level looks like** — concrete behaviours/examples per level
- **How to improve** — specific actions to move up a level

---

## What to Build

A single-page HTML app where:
- Team members are rows, skills are columns (grouped by category)
- Click a cell to set your level (1–4, colour-coded)
- Click a skill header to expand the detail panel
- Data persists in localStorage
- Can add/remove team members
